About Me

Iko-Ojo is a PhD candidate in the School of Computing at the Australian National University (ANU). His research focuses on Algorithm Debt in Machine and Deep Learning systems, exploring its identification, causes, and mitigation strategies. He has a strong background in software engineering, data science, and AI, with experience in technical debt analysis. In addition to his research, Iko-Ojo is actively involved in academia as a tutor and reviewer, contributing to the broader computing and AI research community.
